📍 Scavenger Hunt

Find checkpoint QR codes and claim them in any order.

How to Play:
  • Open the main game link or scan the main QR code to begin.
  • Walk around and find checkpoint QR codes.
  • Each checkpoint QR loads its checkpoint automatically.
  • Tap Claim Checkpoint to record it.
  • Each checkpoint only counts once on the same device.
  • When all 12 are found, show your completion screen to staff.
Progress stays on this device for 24 hours unless you reset it sooner.
